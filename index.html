Our Galaxy

There are so many galaxy that human know
until now.

Milky-Way Galaxy

Is a galaxy where we live and it composed with a lot of stars, cosmic fog, and dust which are distributed. We can see the stars in the galaxy with our eyes in the night and the center of the galaxy is really bright. Around the center of this  galaxy there is star and stellar material that form into a plate.

Blue Pinwheel Galaxy

This galaxy has a round shape like a wheel with a bright blue color. This galaxy is a neighbor to the Milky-Way galaxy so that can easily seen by human with telescope.

Andromeda Galaxy

Andromeda or Messier 31 has become well known by people because can be seen without telescope and it also the same with Milky Way, because its spiral and the distance is 2.5 million light years.
